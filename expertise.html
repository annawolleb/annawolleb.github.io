Aree di competenza Areas of expertise

Disturbi del linguaggioLanguage disorders

Valutazione e trattamento del Disturbo Specifico del Linguaggio (DSL) e delle difficoltà nella comprensione e produzione verbale in età evolutiva.

Assessment and treatment of Specific Language Impairment (DSL) and difficulties with comprehension and expressive language in children.

Comunicazione e spettro autisticoCommunication & autism spectrum

Intervento sulle difficoltà linguistiche secondarie al Disturbo dello Spettro Autistico e sullo sviluppo della comunicazione intenzionale.

Intervention for language difficulties secondary to Autism Spectrum Disorder, and support for the development of intentional communication.

Assessment, prescription support, and consultation for Augmentative and Alternative Communication devices, within an evidence-based intervention.

Distinguishing typical bilingual development from language disorder, profiling language skills across languages, and advising families and schools.
